Me and My Friends

Project Overview

This project explores personal memories through stories about friendship. Each page focuses on a different friend and a shared experience and the reader moves through the narrative by clicking links.

Rather than telling one continuous story, I broke the story into smaller moments focusing on some key experiences me and my friends have had that often come up in our conversations as jokes.

Concept & Idea

Rather than directly describing myself I chose to describe experiences I've had with close friends which show small inside jokes.

Each “Friend” page acts as a snapshot of a memory. Some of them are complete and lighthearted, while others are unresolved, showing how friendships and experiences continue to evolve over time.
