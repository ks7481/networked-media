Friend A

A memory we always go back to

It was after school on a half day and we were waiting for our other friend.
We decided to go to the corner store to get snacks while waiting.
It was hot — at least 80 degrees — so when we walked in and saw the Icee machines, our minds were made immediately.
We walk into the area where the machines were and grab our cups. There were four flavors.
Too many options.
My friend wanted to try one of them, but she didn’t want to commitment in case she didn’t like it. Instead of using a cup like a normal person, she decided to put a little bit of the Icee directly into her hand.
That was mistake number one.
She tried it and immediately got a brain freeze.
We both started laughing.
And then she choked.
For about five minutes, we were laughing, panicking, and trying to clean up at the same time. Eventually, everything was fine, and we finally got our Icees.
As we left the store and crossed the street back toward the school, we joked that she was probably going to get another brain freeze.
She laughed.
Then took a sip.
And immediately got another brain freeze.
