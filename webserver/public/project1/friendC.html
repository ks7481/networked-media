Friend C

A silly experience that stays in the back of our minds

We were getting lunch after an art program we did over the weekend.
About halfway through the meal, my friend said she would signal the waiter so we could get the check.
I agreed.
Time passed.
We kept eating and talking.
Every time the waiter walked out from the bar or the kitchen, she froze. No eye contact. No movement.
I reminded her the next time the waiter walked by.
Same result.
At this point, it became a game. I wanted to see how long it would take her to actually ask for the check.
We finished eating.
Then ten minutes passed.
Then twenty.
Then thirty.
The waiter walked by multiple times. Every time, she panicked and looked everywhere except at him.
After forty minutes, I finally gave up and asked for the check myself.
From that point on, it became a running joke between us of her inability to make eye contact when it mattered most.
